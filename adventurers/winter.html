Winter activities

Build your weekend around calm snow-day experiences and scenic high-country views.

  • Guided snowshoe walks at first light
  • Evening cocoa by the fire lounge
  • Photography stops along the overlook ridge
  • Cabin game nights and slow mornings with mountain views

Best time to go

Fresh snowfall and crisp visibility usually make mid-winter the most scenic part of the season, while early spring still offers snow on the peaks with slightly easier road access.
